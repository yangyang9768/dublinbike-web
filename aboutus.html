about us

Dublin Bikes

Dublin Bikes is a public bicycle rental scheme, which has operated in the city of Dublin since 2009. We now have over 1000 bicycles and 58 stations in the Dublin area. Dublin Bikes was considered one of the most successful bike sharing schemes in the world.

where can you find us?

Dublin Bikes stations are distributed in close proximity to each other in the Dublin City Centre, so you are never far away from renting or returning a bike. view our station
